OUR STORY

LegalSearch is India's #1 Startup Enablement and Legal Services Platform. We support aspiring entrepreneurs withbusiness setup, trademark protection, ROC filing, Tax filing and funding legal support.

We are on a mission to make India an entrepreneurial capital of the world by 2047 through our robust Startup and SME legal services.

During our decade-old journey, LegalSearch has served small businesses across the length and breadth of India,starting from Kargil to Kanyakumari and Bhuj in Gujrat to Tinsukia in Assam.
